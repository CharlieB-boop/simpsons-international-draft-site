About Simpsons

International removals,
done properly

At Simpsons we provide a professional international removal and shipping service throughout the UK, the majority of Europe and the most popular worldwide destinations. Whether you are a returning citizen or moving overseas for business or lifestyle, we have tailor-made services to assist you.

Our services range from shipping and airfreight for smaller consignments of boxes and suitcases, to partial and full household moves. The expertise of our moving team helps you choose the right service — and ensures your personal possessions are packed carefully and arrive safely at your new home.

Your move manager

International removals can feel complicated, especially when you are trying to coordinate timelines, paperwork, packing and delivery from another country. Simpsons keeps that process personal, giving you one dedicated move manager who understands your move and keeps the details joined up.

Packing & Materials

Packed properly, from the first box to the last

Every move starts with careful packing. Choose our full export packing service and our crews will wrap, box and load your entire home for you — or take care of it yourself with export-grade cartons, tape and bubble wrap sourced directly from us.

Treasured pieces need extra attention. For antiques, artwork and other irreplaceable items, we build bespoke crates and cases around the object itself, so nothing shifts in transit.

We recommend booking 6–8 weeks before your intended move date, particularly for international sea freight where sailing schedules are fixed well in advance. For UK and European road moves, 2–4 weeks' notice is usually enough — though we'll always do what we can to accommodate shorter timeframes.

Every quote is based on a free home or video survey, taking into account the volume of your belongings, your destination, and the shipping method — sea, air or road. There are no hidden fees; your quotation covers packing materials, transport and the services we agree together.

Yes. All shipments are covered by comprehensive marine transit insurance for the full replacement value of your goods, giving you complete peace of mind from collection right through to delivery.

Our team handles all customs documentation on your behalf and will advise you on any duties or import restrictions specific to your destination country, so there are no surprises when your shipment arrives.

Transit times vary by route and method — typically 5–10 weeks for sea freight, and 1–2 weeks for air freight or European road moves. We'll confirm an estimated timeline for your specific move as part of your quotation.
