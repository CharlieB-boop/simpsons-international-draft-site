Five things we will not compromise on

No jargon and no small print. These are the commitments behind every quotation we send, and you can hold us to each of them.

A coordinator who knows your name

One person looks after your move. They answer the phone on the day you enquire and they are still there on the day your boxes arrive. Our customers mention their coordinators by name in reviews, and that tells you everything about how we like to work.

A single point of contact for your whole move

Two

Packing treated as a craft

Our crews pack for a living, and it shows. Professional grade materials, export wrapping for furniture, and the patience to do the fiddly things properly. The measure of a good packer is simple. Everything arrives exactly as it left.

Export grade materials on every shipment

Three

Trusted teams on both sides of the water

An international move is only as good as the crew at the far end. We work with established destination partners we know personally, so the standard you see at your front door is the standard you get at your new one.

Established partners in every destination we serve

Four

Insured at every stage

Accidents are rare, and we plan for them anyway. Comprehensive marine transit insurance is available on every shipment, underwritten by Basil Fry, one of the UK’s leading removals insurers. Cover starts at packing and runs until delivery, with up to 30 days of storage included.

Cover to full destination replacement value

Straight answers on price and timing

A free home or video survey means your quotation reflects what is actually moving, not a guess. We tell you realistic transit times before you book, and we put the details in writing so there are no surprises later.

Free home and video surveys, no obligation
