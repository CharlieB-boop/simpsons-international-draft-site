The Move

Singapore is one of the world's most efficient ports, and one of our smoothest routes.

British professionals relocate to Singapore every year for work, and the city's efficient customs process makes it one of our more straightforward long-haul moves. We manage the shipping and paperwork so settling in feels easy.

Sea freight suits a full household, and air freight covers anything you would rather have on arrival.

Your options

Choose the right way to ship

Every move to Singapore is different, so we shape the service around yours.

Sea · Sole-use

Your own 20ft or 40ft container

Your belongings travel alone, packed and sealed at your door and not opened again until they reach you. The fastest and most secure sea option.

Best for: full household moves
Sea · Shared

Groupage / part-load container

Your goods share container space with other shipments on the same route, splitting the cost between several moves. Slightly longer transit, but excellent value.

Best for: smaller moves, flexible budgets
Air Freight

Fast-track for essentials

Air freight suits the things you need soon after you land, such as documents, clothing and everyday items. It arrives in a fraction of the time.

Best for: essentials & smaller shipments
Excess Baggage

A few boxes or suitcases

Not moving a whole household? Our excess baggage service is a simple, affordable way to send personal items and smaller consignments overseas.

Best for: light movers & students
Good to know

Singapore customs is efficient, with a few distinctive rules worth knowing

A valid work pass or long-term visa generally qualifies you for GST relief on used personal and household effects. Singapore also has a handful of rules that surprise first-time movers, and we flag these before you pack.

Work pass relief

A valid work pass or long-term visa usually qualifies your used household goods for GST relief.

Chewing gum & a few surprises

Singapore restricts a small number of everyday items, including chewing gum, more tightly than most countries.

Electronics & new goods

GST is payable on new and high value items, so keep your receipts to hand.

Inventory & valuation

A detailed, valued inventory keeps your customs clearance straightforward.
